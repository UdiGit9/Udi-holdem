Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 23, 2026

This is the privacy policy for Udi's Hold'em Trainer, a free poker training web app available at udiholdem.com and as an installable app on the Google Play Store. This policy explains what we collect, why, and what your choices are. The app is operated by an individual developer (referred to here as "we").

What we collect

We intentionally collect as little as possible. The only data tied to you:

  • Leaderboard entries — when you finish a 20-hand test and choose to save your score, we store the display name you type, your score, and the timestamp of the test. You can skip this step and still use the app normally.
  • Local preferences — your chosen app mode, past names you've used, and your per-device leaderboard are stored in your browser's local storage. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

We do not collect your email, phone number, IP address, precise location, advertising ID, contacts, photos, or any device identifiers. We do not use analytics or tracking pixels. There are no ads.

Tips (optional)

The app includes an optional "Ante" tip jar. Tips are processed by Stripe, Inc. on their hosted checkout page. We never see your card number or billing address; Stripe shares with us only the transaction amount, the last four digits of your card, and a timestamp, for our own bookkeeping. Stripe's privacy practices are governed by Stripe's privacy policy.

Who we share data with

Leaderboard entries are stored on JSONBin.io, a third-party key-value hosting service, so the leaderboard can be shared across devices. JSONBin receives the data you voluntarily submit (name, score, timestamp) and nothing else. JSONBin's practices are governed by their privacy policy.

We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers. We do not use it to build a profile of you.

Cookies and local storage

We do not set any cookies. We do use your browser's local storage to remember your settings and local leaderboard. You can clear this any time from your browser's settings.

Data retention and deletion

Leaderboard entries are displayed for the current week (rolling 7-day window) and then automatically drop off the displayed list. The underlying records are pruned periodically. If you want a specific entry removed sooner, email us (below) with the name and approximate timestamp you used, and we'll delete it.

Children

This app is rated Teen / 12+ due to depiction of card games. It is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted a leaderboard entry, contact us and we will remove it.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete the data we hold about you (for example under GDPR if you are in the EU, or under the CCPA if you are in California). Since we only hold optional leaderboard entries, exercising these rights is simple: email us and we'll handle the request.

Security

All traffic to and from the app is over HTTPS. No system is perfectly secure, but given how little data we collect, the risk surface is small by design.

Changes to this policy

If we materially change what we collect or how we use it, we'll update the "effective date" above and, for significant changes, post a notice in the app.

Contact

Questions, deletion requests, or anything else: udi.hoffmann@gmail.com.
